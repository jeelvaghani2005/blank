About Us

Welcome to SoniтАЩs Phonics

At Sonic's Phonics, we believe that mastering spoken English can be both fun and educational.

Our mission is to provide high-quality, engaging, and interactive spoken English classes tailored specifically for kids. We understand the unique ways children learn and aim to foster a love for language through dynamic and enjoyable lessons.

Goal

Our Goal

"Our goal in phonics classes is to help students master the relationship between sounds and letters, enabling them to read and write with confidence and fluency. By developing strong decoding, blending, and phonemic awareness skills, we aim to create a solid foundation for lifelong literacy and learning."
